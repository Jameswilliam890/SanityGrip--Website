Why We Created Sanity Grip

We came up with the idea of a 3D-printed door handle for a few important reasons. Many people, especially those with germophobia, feel uncomfortable touching door handles in public places. This is especially true in high-traffic areas like bathrooms, where hygiene is a concern. Our design minimises direct contact to reduce germ spread and provide peace of mind.

With 3D printing, we could create something both useful and good-looking. Our project shows how creative ideas and tech can make everyday life better.

Materials & Design Tool

We used Makers Empire to design and create our product. It’s a fun and powerful app that helps you bring your ideas to life through 3D printing. It was perfect for our school project and allowed us to be creative and practical.
